Four thrilling titles from the Metropolitan Opera’s award-winning Live in HD series return to theaters this summer. Experience legendary Met performances from some of opera’s greatest stars, including sopranos Renée Fleming and Sondra Radvanovsky, mezzo-soprano Isabel Leonard, and baritone Dmitri Hvorostovsky!

Schedule in the United States and Canada*

PHILIP GLASS

Akhnaten

IN CINEMAS JULY 26

Countertenor Anthony Roth Costanzo stars as the revolutionary pharaoh in the 2019 company premiere of Philip Glass’s mesmerizing modern masterpiece.

VERDI

Il Trovatore

IN CINEMAS AUGUST 2

David McVicar’s action-packed production returns in a gripping performance from 2011, featuring soprano Sondra Radvanovsky, tenor Marcelo Álvarez, and baritone Dmitri Hvorostovsky. 

ROSSINI

Il Barbiere di Siviglia

IN CINEMAS AUGUST 9

Mezzo-soprano Isabel Leonard, tenor Lawrence Brownlee, and baritone Christopher Maltman join forces in this delightful performance of Rossini’s madcap comedy from 2014. 

TCHAIKOVSKY

Eugene Onegin

IN CINEMAS AUGUST 16

Beloved soprano Renée Fleming and baritone Dmitri Hvorostovsky come together in this poetic drama of unrequited love, recorded live from the Met in 2007. 

*Encore titles and dates may vary at select independent theaters and international venues. Please check your local cinema listings.

2023–24 Live in HD Season

The 2023–24 Live in HD season begins in October with an extraordinary lineup of company premieres, exciting new productions, and classic revivals. Tickets go on sale July 26 in the U.S. and Canada.
